About me

Pedro Proença (English pronunciation: Peh-dru Pru-ensuh) is a computer vision wizard, robotocist and surfer from Portugal. He currently works at NASA-JPL developing perception and state estimation software to support autonomy for future robotic space missions. His PhD was on Probabilistic Visual Odometry using RGB-D and Geometric primitives for Man-made Environments.

He is particularly interested in modelling uncertainty and using multiple view geometry, deep learning and computer graphics to solve robotics and computer vision problems.
